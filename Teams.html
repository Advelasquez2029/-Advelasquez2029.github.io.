Japan National Team

The Japanese volleyball team is famous for its discipline, precise plays, and fast offense. Known as the "Nippon Volley," they have consistently ranked among the world's best teams and are known for their signature quick sets and defense.

Alas Pilipinas

Alas Pilipinas is the official Philippine women’s volleyball team, proudly representing the country in international competitions. Their dedication and energy have inspired a new generation of volleyball fans in the Philippines.

Creamline Cool Smashers

Creamline is one of the most popular professional volleyball teams in the Philippines, known for their strong chemistry and teamwork. Their pink jerseys and loyal fanbase make them a staple in the PVL.

PLDT High Speed Hitters

PLDT’s team is known for its power hitters and relentless determination. With each season, they continue to improve and compete fiercely in the Premier Volleyball League.

Karasuno High (Haikyuu!!)

Though fictional, Karasuno High has become iconic thanks to the anime Haikyuu!! — inspiring millions of volleyball fans worldwide. Their story of teamwork, perseverance, and "flying" again resonates with players everywhere.
